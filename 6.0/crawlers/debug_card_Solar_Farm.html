Info

Solar Farm is a large Dooley item in The Bazaar. Its starting tier is Gold. As Dooley, you can find this item at Jay Jay, Barkun, Tok's Clocks, Kina, Midsworth, Luxe, Goldie, The Tester, Chronos, Hef and Pol. You can also rarely find this item enchanted at Cobweb, Knightshade and Freiya. Solar Farm can also be dropped by Ahexa. A full list of available enchantments for Solar Farm can be found below. Deep Mechanics for Solar Farm are also available below.
