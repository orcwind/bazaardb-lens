Info

Crypto is a small Dooley item in The Bazaar. Its starting tier is Bronze. As Dooley, you can find this item at Jay Jay, Kina, Midsworth, Silvia, Luxe, Goldie, The Tester, Quixel and Ande. Crypto can also be dropped by Mod Squad and Ahexa. A full list of available enchantments for Crypto can be found below. Deep Mechanics for Crypto are also available below.
